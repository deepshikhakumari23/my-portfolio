01. ABOUT ME

Engineering the Future of Hardware.

Hello! I'm Deep Shikha Kumari, a 3rd-year Electronics and Communication Engineering student with a passion for building efficient, low-level systems.

My journey started with a fascination for how code interacts with physical circuits. Today, I specialize in STM32 Microcontrollers and Embedded C, focusing on writing firmware that is as close to the metal as possible.

When I'm not debugging timers or SPI protocols, I'm exploring VLSI design and Semiconductor Physics, aiming to bridge the gap between high-level logic and high-performance hardware.
